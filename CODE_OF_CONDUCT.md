# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to making participation in this project a welcoming experience for everyone, regardless of background, experience level, or identity.

## Our Standards

**Encouraged:**
- Constructive feedback and respectful disagreement
- Focusing on what is best for the project and community
- Showing empathy toward other contributors

**Not acceptable:**
- Harassment, trolling, or personal attacks
- Publishing others' private information without consent
- Sustained disruptive behavior in issues, PRs, or discussions

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies in all project spaces — GitHub issues, pull requests, and discussions.

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported to **sparmeet162000@gmail.com**. All reports will be reviewed and responded to promptly. Maintainers have the right to remove comments, close issues, or ban contributors who violate this code.
